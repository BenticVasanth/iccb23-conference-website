International Conference on Cancer Biology

Molecular Mechanisms, Genomics and Novel Therapeutics

September 14th to 16th - 2023,
Venue: T.T.J. Auditorium, ICSR, IIT Madras, Chennai-600036.

Welcome to ICCB23

We have great pleasure in inviting you to the
“International Conference on Cancer Biology: Molecular Mechanisms, Genomics and Novel Therapeutics”

Scope: There are unprecedented scientific and clinical advances in understanding , diagnosis and treatment of cancer and this conference will be highlighting the developments in managing and treating human cancers.

Audience: Oncologists, specialist clinicians dealing with cancer, general practitioners, epidemiologists, pathologists, medical students, cancer researchers, graduate students and post-docs, who wish to expand their knowledge of new strategies in molecular pathology and management of cancer and therapeutics.
